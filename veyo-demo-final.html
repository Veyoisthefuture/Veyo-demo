II.The Framework

Six stages, transposed.

What Hermès evaluates in a boutique — applied to a creator's subscriber journey.

I.

Be A Host

Hermès · Boutique
Browse until approached. If no one approaches in five minutes, that is a scored failure. Recognition without intrusion. Hospitality before transaction.
Veyo · Creator
Welcome message, first proactive DM, free preview timing. 48 hours of silence = Hermès advisor walking past a client for five minutes. Scored the same way.
II.

Build A Connection

Hermès · Boutique
The advisor discovers needs through questions. Taste, occasion, relationship with the brand. The client book is built through curiosity, not transaction.
Veyo · Creator
Subscriber discovery. What brought them, what they value, what they've responded to before. The Profiler agent constructs the creator's client book — one dossier per subscriber, exactly as an Hermès advisor would.
III.

Inspire

Rolex · Acne Studios · Stone Island
Was the advisor passionate? What did they say about brand values? Passion is explicitly scored. Generic enthusiasm fails.
Veyo · Creator
Brand storytelling layer of every interaction. Does content carry universe and values, or only transaction? The Composer generates concepts that encode brand passion explicitly.
IV.

Reveal

Hermès · Tiffany · Bottega Veneta
The product is staged, not displayed. How was it presented? How did it make you feel? The reveal is ritual, not transaction.
Veyo · Creator
PPV reveals, exclusive drops, tier upgrades — staged as ritual. The Oracle and Composer coordinate: scarcity as theater, not throughput.
V.

See Long-term

Hermès · Saint Laurent · Acne
Did the advisor introduce additional items? Invite future visits? The transaction was incidental. The relationship continues.
Veyo · Creator
Tier upgrades, VIP relationship architecture, custom content. The difference between a subscriber and a client is whether the system sees beyond the next PPV.
VI.

After Your Visit

Saint Laurent 5d · Acne 3d · Dior 15d
Every brand has a codified follow-up SLA. Failure to recontact within the window is a scored failure, regardless of how strong the visit was.
Veyo · Creator
Cadenced re-engagement per creator brand. No creator on the platform currently has a follow-up SLA. This is the most under-served and highest-ROI stage.
